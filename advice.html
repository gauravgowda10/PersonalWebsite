Advice for Startup Students:

My main advice for startup students is to use the resources available to you at the Allen School. If you need help, do not feel scared to ask for help. There should be no reason for you to have to work by youself on an assignment. Make sure to find a study group where you can be productive, and that will help you achieve your goals within your claseses.
